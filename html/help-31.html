The fastest, simplest way to travel and share culture.

A friendly way to get there

If you're looking for a fast way of finding an affordable mean of traveling, you are at the right place. Search through our database populated with results from the most popular ridesharing services available out there. Find the right driver for you by checking their profile and reviews, and message the ones you like. Arrange your trip, split the gas and save money. We make ridesharing a viable way to travel by providing you with peer reviewed listings and present you with a wide variety of choices when it comes to picking a driver.

Peer to peer ridesharing helps you make new friends and get to your destination safely, while cutting your traveling expenses by over 75%.

A comfy home feeling place to stay

With currently over 400,000 places in over 34,000 cities around the globe, Outpost provides one of the biggest peer to peer rentals database available online. Whether you're looking for a one night apartment booking in the heart of NYC, a house for the weekend or a vacation rental in a luxurious villa in the Caribbean for a week, we got you covered. Search fast through the results using the available filters and find the right match for you.

You can then follow-up with the provider and rent the chosen place instantly. Now all you got left to do is pack up and go!

A way to share culture and discover new experiences

We have found you a way to get there, a place to spend the night in, now it's time to find some fun activities to do while you're there. We've put together a list of culture enriching activities offered by habitants of the place you're about to visit. Get the full experience of the city you'll be in. Take advice from the locals and discover secrets of their area that you would never get to experience in a commercial tour.

Whether it's a culinary tour of the best secret places kept in town, discovering amazing street art and culture, or learning a history lesson that you're looking for, they are all here waiting for you.
