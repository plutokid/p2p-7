About Outpost

We'd like to start by saying that Outpost was built in less than 4 days just to get a spontaneous idea out of our minds and into code. That said, we applied to YCombinator's summer 2013 cohort, and wanted to see if connecting P2P websites is something that people want before pursuing Outpost any further.

Sorry if there are bugs or no information available next in your neighborhood, we are adding more P2P networks every day so check again soon!

Usage

Outpost is meant to be used for people who want to take P2P more seriously by planning their trip using such services in one place:
  1. Input your current location
  2. Where do you want to go ?
  3. When are you planning to leave and when are you planning to check out ?
